milkquartet

Katherine Fortunato. Maddy Dethloff. Lauren Molloy. Nupur Thakkar.

milkquartet is a percussion group that is focused on commissioning new works for percussion as well as educating younger generations of percussionists and working with communities through outreach. One of the only all-female percussion groups in the world, mmilk strives to bring representation to minorities in and beyond the music community. The group is currently working with Sandbox Percussion, Brooklyn-based percussion quartet, in a two-year long mentorship program.
