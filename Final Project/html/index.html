mmilk is a percussion quartet that is focused on commissioning new works for percussion as well as educating younger generations of percussionists and working with communities through outreach. One of the only all-female percussion groups in the world, mmilk strives to bring representation to minorities in and beyond the music community. The group is currently working with Sandbox Percussion, Brooklyn-based percussion quartet, in a two-year long mentorship program.

Our Goals:

  • Commission the music of minorities and under-represented peoples in music (i.e. LGBTQ+ Community, female/non-binary composers).

  • Community outreach through workshops, masterclasses, education.

  • Provide educational opportunities in areas with little arts funding.
